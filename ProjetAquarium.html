Connected aquarium

Aquarium of our former client

During this project, we had to create a connected aquarium for a real client, this aquarium had to give food to the fish, control the light in the aquarium, and manage the temperature of the aquarium
The solution was to create an app connected to a raspberry (inside the aquarium), who was collecting data, all this data was displaying on the app, and you could modify these parameters on the app, and control your aquarium.
